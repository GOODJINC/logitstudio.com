Alias Flow is a Flow Launcher plugin that supports user-defined aliases and Korean initial consonant search. It maximizes workflow efficiency by launching complex program paths and lengthy website URLs with just a few keystrokes. It offers various features and conveniences including Korean initial consonant search, multi-alias mapping, zero-dependency architecture, unified launcher, and easy backup.
